Sustainability is the new luxury

When luxury meets sustainability and is adorned with unique details, marvelous solutions come to life, like this personalized business card.

An object that in a small space aims to convey a multitude of meanings through various communication channels, creating a multisensory experience.

The tactile effect created by the raised texture of Corvon Rust paper stimulates the sense of touch, inviting people to linger on this pleasant sensation.

Attention to detail, such as the gold hot stamping of the logo in the center, captivates the sight, noticing a detail that shines and embellishes the business card.

The thickness of the business card itself, resulting from the combination of three types of paper, communicates importance and presence.

The choice of precious, certified papers, derived from upcycling processes, speaks to the commitment to sustainability.
